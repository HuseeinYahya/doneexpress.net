Fastest Safest Destination for Shipping & E-commerce

Done provides integrated logistics solutions for e-commerce stores, including product sourcing, shipping, and storage. Done will contribute to creating opportunities for expansion in e-commerce through its system for managing shipping and delivery operations to customers via a wide network of pickup and delivery points.

Why Done?

  • Professional and reliable shipping & delivery services
  • Safe storage in well-equipped locations
  • Faster shipping times for better service quality
  • Easy tracking with API integration
  • Shipping from outside Yemen (Battel service)
  • Cash-on-delivery payments settled within 14 days
